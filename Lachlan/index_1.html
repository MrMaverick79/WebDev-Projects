Explore Jaisalmer

Known as India's 'Golden City', Jaisalmer has something for everyone. At its heart is the Golden Fort, which changes colours throughout the day and turns golden as the sun sets. The city sits on the edge of the Thar Desert, home to an abundance of wildlife. We can show you the secrets that abound!
